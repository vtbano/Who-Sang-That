Who sang that?

Do you ever have that moment where you hear a tune and you just can't recall Who Sang That?
This game does just that, but eliminates those helpful hints like the voice, the beat, and the sound. You have 10 rounds that display the lytics and 3 different artists to choose from as the perfect pair.
Are you up to this 10 round challenge that tests you top hits knowledge?
