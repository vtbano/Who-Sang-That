Who sang that?

Do you ever have that moment where you hear a tune and you just can't recall Who Sang That? This game does just that, but eliminates those helpful hints like the voice, the beat, and the sound. You have 10 rounds that display the lyrics and 3 different artists to choose from as the perfect pair.

Are you for this 10 round challenge that tests your top hits knowledge?
